About Me

I’m a Chicago-based journalist pursuing a master’s in investigative journalism at Northwestern University's Medill School as a Paul Sagan ProPublica Medill Investigative Lab Scholar.

I'm a Brit, a bookworm and a Brown University alumna who spent the last three years working for daily newspapers in Maine, covering everything from town meetings and cheese festivals to contractor fraud and a mass shooting. I have won two 1st place awards from the Maine Press Association for my writing.

My best work, and the work I feel best about, is that which helps my readers make informed decisions and creates impact in my community. I'm passionate about writing watchdog stories that keep government honest and expose bad actors who exploit vulnerable people.
